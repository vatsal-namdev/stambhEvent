About the Evenet

The poetry & essay writing event in college is an opportunity for students to showcase their literary talents and express their thoughts and ideas through writing. It provides a platform for budding writers to share their work with their peers and receive feedback from experienced faculty members and guest writers. During the event, students can submit their original poems and essays for consideration, and a selection of the best pieces will be chosen to be read or presented at the event. This presents an opportunity for students to gain recognition for their creative writing abilities and to connect with others who share their passion for literature.
